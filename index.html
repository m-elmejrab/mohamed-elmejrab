Film & TV Projects
Selected works in production and color grading
Marionette TV Series

Role: Production Manager
Description: "Marionette" is a powerful Libyan drama series that delves into themes of power, loyalty, and moral ambiguity in a nation struggling with its future. The story follows Omar, the elusive leader of "Almajma'," as he challenges the status quo and sparks conflict with the ruling government.
As Production Manager, I oversaw the entire production process of the 10-episode series, leading a team of over 55 cast and crew members. Despite the logistical and security complexities of filming in Libya, I successfully delivered the full series on time and within budget, completing principal photography in just 42 days. The series was broadcast on Libya Al-Ahrar TV and reached millions of viewers on air and across digital platforms, marking a significant achievement in contemporary Libyan television production.
